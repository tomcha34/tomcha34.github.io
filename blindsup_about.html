BlindsUp! App

Set your poker tournament, your way.

BlindsUp! is an app made for poker players looking to host their own poker tournaments. This app allows users to create the blinds structure for their tournament, and also set the time of each level. The app also has a widget feature to see how much time is left in the current level. When the level ends, an audio alert let's everyone know that Blinds are Up! you can simply shake your phone to stop the alert.
