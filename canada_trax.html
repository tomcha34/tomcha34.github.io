Canada Trax App

COVID-19 - Keep up to date and stay safe

We felt an application was needed to help Canadians during a very difficult time due to the pandemic. We created the Canada Trax app to allow users to view COVID-19 case numbers, and to also be able to keep track of close contacts if they happen to test postive for COVID-19.

Collaboration: Myself, and 2 fellow students.

Language: Kotlin

Operating System: Android

Technologies and Frameworks used:

  • Kotlin
  • XML
  • Retrofit
  • Co-Routines
  • SQLite
  • GIT
  • OKHTTP

Our Colour Scheme

Font-Family: New Times Roman

How it started

The CanadaTrax app features a straightforward UX system. We expect our users to often be on the go when using our app, and they'll want to get to the required information as quickly as possible. CanadaTrax has all the functionality you need to find out recent COVID-19 data, and also quickly add any close contacts.
